My story

From soil to screen.

A short journey — why I study horticulture, why I design, and where I’m headed.

01

Why horticulture

I joined Dr. YSR Horticulture University with a dream already planted: a beautiful farm — livestock on open land, flowers in colour, landscaping shaped with care. Not just a course. A life I could see.

02

Still studying, still growing

I’m still a student — learning soil, plants, and landscape while that farm picture gets clearer every semester. The passion hasn’t changed; the roots are going deeper.

03

Why graphic design

Design became how I show the dream before it’s real — flyers, colour, type, layout. The same eye that plans a field plans a poster. I create visuals at Bassik Hospitality Services Pvt Ltd (graphic designer, leading role · from April 2025) while I keep studying — two paths, one creative heart.
