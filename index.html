Design at Bassik

Flyers, colour, type — that’s my everyday language. At Bassik Hospitality I listen to the brief, sketch fast, and shape visuals people actually stop for.

Dr. YSR Horticulture University

Horticulture at Dr. YSR Horticulture University. I walked in with a dream — open land, flowers, livestock, a farm cared for with patience. Lectures, labs, and time on campus — I’m building knowledge I want to use for real.

03
